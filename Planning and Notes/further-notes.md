### Different deck views

The deckbuilder will have several different ways to display the deck, each optimized around a certain way of interacting with the decklist.

**Card Stacks** - Probably the most overall intuitive view. Useful for manipulating cards as physical objects. You can see your mana curve, colors, etc and get a good feel for the overall qualities of the deck.

**Visual Spoiler** - The best view if you want to read all the cards.

**Table** - Useful for editing card data (changing mana value, color id, printings, etc.)

**List** – Provides a quick way to read through the entire decklist. Useful for editing the decklist (adding/removing cards) and import/export.

**Stats** - I'm not sold that this is strictly necessary, but some people might want to see the stats on mana values/curve, color breakdown, type breakdown, etc.

**Playtest** – Also lower priority. I'm not going to build a full playtest engine, but I do want to allow users to:
 - Draw sample hand
 - Mulligan (i.e. draw new hand & put one on bottom)
- Draw a card
